Local Excursions

Join your colleagues for these planned social and cultural outings in the Durham area:

Sarah P. Duke Gardens

A guided walking tour of one of the premier public gardens in the United States, located right on Duke's campus.

Durham Bulls Athletic Park

Experience a classic American afternoon at the ballpark, home of the world-famous Durham Bulls.

American Tobacco Campus

A tour of Durham's historic revitalized district, featuring unique architecture, shops, and dining.

Travel to Durham

Raleigh-Durham International (RDU) is the primary airport, located ~15 miles from the venue.

  • Uber/Lyft: ~$24 - $35
  • Taxi: ~$42 - $52
  • Parking: Included for hotel guests; $15/day for off-site attendees.
