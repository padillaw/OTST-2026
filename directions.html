Getting to Durham, NC

By Air

The closest major airport is Raleigh-Durham International Airport (RDU). The airport is located approximately 15 miles (20-25 minutes by car) from the conference venue.

Transportation options from RDU to the JB Duke Hotel:

  • Taxi: Available at the airport terminals. Estimated cost: $42 - $52 USD. (Taxi Taxi of Raleigh)
  • Ride-sharing (Uber/Lyft): Available through their respective apps. Estimated cost: $24 - $35 USD. (Uber Price Estimator)
  • Rental Cars: Several rental car agencies operate at RDU.

Parking

For guests staying at the JB Duke Hotel, parking is included with your room. For conference attendees not staying at the hotel, parking is available at a rate of $15 USD per day.
