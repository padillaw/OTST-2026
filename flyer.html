Featured Information

The 11th International Conference on Optical Terahertz Science and Technology (OTST 2026) will unite researchers to discuss the latest advancements in THz research, from basic science to new technology. The event will be hosted by the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering in the Pratt School of Engineering at Duke University.

Conference Topics:

  • Generation and Detection
  • Spectroscopy
  • Far-field and Near-field Imaging Techniques
  • Ultrafast dynamics & Nonlinear Phenomena
  • Materials
  • Artificial electromagnetic materials
  • Semiconductor Physics and Devices
  • Quantum Optics and Phenomena
  • Communications and Systems
  • Biology and Biomedical Applications
  • Artificial Intelligence and Signal Processing
  • Industrial Applications

Organizers:

  • Chairs: Willie Padilla (Duke University) and Hassan Arbab (Stony Brook University)
  • Technical Committee: Hou-tong Chen (Los Alamos National Laboratory) and Michael Johnston (University of Oxford)
