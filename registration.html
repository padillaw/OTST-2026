Abstract Submission

The Abstract Submission Deadline is January 7th, 2026.

Submission Guidelines

  • Abstracts must be prepared using the official one-page template: Download Abstract Template (.DOC).
  • The final submission must be a single PDF file. Only PDF files will be accepted.
  • IMPORTANT: The submission form will guide you to upload your PDF via a secure Duke Box link (no Google sign-in required). You must confirm successful upload in the form.
  • Upon successful submission of the form, you will receive a confirmation email containing your individual paper number. Please check your spam folder.
  • If you wish to attend the conference, please remember that abstract submission is separate from conference registration. Register on the Registration page.

Required Information for Submission

The online form will ask for the following information, which we recommend you prepare in advance:

  • Abstract Title
  • Presenting Author Details: Email, First Name, Last Name, Organization, City, and Country.
  • Your Presentation Preference (Talk, Poster, or No Preference).
  • Your primary Conference Topic Area (e.g., Spectroscopy, Materials, Communications).
  • A complete list of Co-Authors and their affiliations.
  • You will need to provide confirmation that your one-page abstract as a PDF File has been uploaded.
